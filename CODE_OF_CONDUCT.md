# Code of Conduct

YAI-Excel is part of the **Hackknow** and **YAHAVIS AI** ecosystem. We follow a short, plainly-stated standard:

## We expect

- Respectful, professional communication
- Good-faith assumption about other contributors' intent
- Constructive feedback focused on the work, not the person
- Patience with newcomers — first-time contributors are welcome here

## We won't tolerate

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks of any kind
- Discriminatory language (race, gender, religion, caste, sexuality, ability, nationality)
- Doxing, threatening to dox, or sharing private info without consent
- Sustained disruption of discussions, issues, or PRs
- Solicitation, spam, or off-topic promotion

## Enforcement

Maintainers will warn first for unintentional minor violations. Repeat or serious violations result in temporary or permanent removal from the project.

Report incidents privately to **conduct@hackknow.com**. All reports are taken seriously and kept confidential.

This document adapts the spirit of the [Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) without the full text bloat.
